About

Jacob Banks is a Nigerian born singer song writer that does not waste any form of his talent as he has dabbled in all creative areas not just msuic. His music career started when he first sung at open mic nights and his soulful voice paired with his very thought provking lyrics quickly created him known in the indsutry.
